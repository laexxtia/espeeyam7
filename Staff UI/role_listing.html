Product Owner

Description

As the Product Owner, you will be responsible for defining and prioritizing a product's features and ensuring they align with customer needs and business goals, and acting as a bridge between stakeholders and the development team to guide the product's direction.

Application Deadline:

Responsibilities

  • Product Strategy:

    Develop and communicate a clear product vision and strategy in collaboration with stakeholders.

  • Backlog Management:

    Maintain a well-defined and prioritized product backlog, ensuring that it reflects current priorities and customer needs.

  • Requirement Gathering:

    Gather and prioritize product requirements from various stakeholders, including customers, internal teams, and market research.

  • User Stories:

    Write detailed user stories, acceptance criteria, and specifications to guide the development team.

  • Cross-functional Collaboration:

    Work closely with cross-functional teams, including development, design, etc, to ensure successful product delivery.

  • Quality Assurance:

    Ensure that the final product meets quality standards and aligns with the initial vision.

Qualifications

  • Proven experience (2 years) as a Product Owner or similar role in a software development environment.
  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Computer Science, Information Systems, Software Engineering, Data Science, or in a related field.
  • Strong understanding of Agile methodologies, Scrum, and/or Kanban.
  • Proficiency in using Agile project management tools such as Jira or Trello.
  • Experience with user-centered design principles and usability testing.
  • Excellent communication, leadership, and teamwork skills.

Salary

Annual income of up to SGD 80,000.
